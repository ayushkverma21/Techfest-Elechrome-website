    Robo Rash Need For Speed

    The motive is to design a wired/wireless being autonomous or manually controlled machine that is capable of completing the ALL TERRAIN Arena successfully in minimum time.

    Robot Specification:
    The dimension of bot should not exceed 30x20x20 cm3 (L*B*H). Non-adherence to the dimensions will lead to outright ousting from the event with no exceptions.
    The minimum width of the bot should be 15 cm.
    The weight of the robot should not exceed 3 Kgs. Weight of adaptors and the remote controller will not be counted.
    The potential difference between any two points on bot must not exceed 24 Volts. The robot can be fully powered only electrically either through power adaptor or through batteries.
    The teams are requested not to use caster wheels as track may contain a bridge with a gap in between. The machine should be controlled by wireless remote controlled mechanism or wired one throughout the race. If the machine is wired then the wire should remain slack under all circumstances during the competition. All the wires coming out of machine should be stacked as a single unit. The wires must be properly insulated. Pulling the wire to aid the robot in traversing may lead to disqualification.
    If RF (Wireless) controller is used, the dual frequency is a must in order to avoid RF jamming. The dual frequency should be such that it can be switched to either frequency just before the start of the run. Ground clearance of robot should be minimum 3 cm.

    Fun Events Have Fun !!

    The fun event consists of activities which will be played for a short amount of time with the participants.

    NAME OF GAMES:


    1. WRESTLING MANIA:
    In this game two participants will compete for each with other, the one who wins will compete with the next. Similarly, five to six people will compete and the strongest among them all wins.
    2. DRAMEBAAZ:
    This game is an act for the Bollywood lovers, the participants can perform in any of the fields of their interest. For example dance, singing, mimicry, etc. The one with the best skills will be chosen as winner.
    3. KAHIN PE NIGAHEIN KAHIN PE NISHANA:
    The game is played in a team of two. Each team will be given a basket and a bunch of balls. They will have to exchange their partners. The person who is from the opposite team will throw the balls by facing in the opposite direction to the other person who will be given a basket. The person with the basket has to catch as much as balls within the area of the circle which will be already marked. The one who catches more number of balls wins.
    4. GRAB IT ALL! :
    The participants will be given thermocol balls scattered around and a straw will be provided, they have to fill the cup provided to them with thermocol balls with the help of the straw by puffing the air, along with the ball on top of the straw with their mouth. The one with more balls in their cup wins the game.
    5. LORD OF RINGS:
    The participants will be provided with rings, they have to aim to the objects at a certain distance set by the volunteers. They have to aim all the five objects with the help of the rings in a single shot respectively. The one who fulfills the criteria wins the game.
    6. DUMB CHARADES:
    This is a well-known game, the participants will have to play a set of five rounds with their respective teams. They will be given Bollywood movies by the volunteers which they have to guess while one of their mates is trying to tell them with the help of their acting skills. The team which wins number of rounds wins.
    7. MASS MURDER:
    The participants will be given a card on which they have to write their respective names. They have to wear the card around their neck and protect it till the end from the other participants who will be trying to snatch i.e. murder them by taking their card. The one with no card will be disqualified. The one who murders more people wins the game.
    8. SAREE K FALL SA:
    In this game, a saree will be provided. The male participant has to wrap his fellow partner and dress him/her beautifully in a saree. The fastest and the precise one wins the game.
